Beginnings

My journey in game development began with the first computer I ever programmed - the ZX81, and the first programming language I learnt - BASIC. Suffice to say that I was not impressed seeing my "player" which was a black square, take ages to make it across the screen. The only solution for it at the time was to dive into assembly language and a very thick book on the Z80 processor if you had the time and the patience

Fast Forward

Since that time obviously a lot has happened. No games development is not my day job but software development is - although I have a good friend who rightly insists that we build solutions not develop software. I develop games and enter into game jams mostly as a hobby. All artwork, graphics, coding (apart from 3rd party libs), sound effects and music I do myself.

I am Arokn Kram and this is MCUZ Productions .. Enter the Wonderful!
